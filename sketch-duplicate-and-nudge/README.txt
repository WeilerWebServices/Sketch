sketch-duplicate-and-nudge
==========================

A port to Sketch.app of Photoshop's Duplicate and Nudge feature

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- ⌥ + arrow keys: duplicate and nudge 1 pixel
- ⌥ + ⇧ + arrow keys: duplicate and nudge 10 pixels

If you need to nudge by any other amount, just edit the .skethplugin files with a text editor, replacing 1 and 10 for your favorite numbers :)
